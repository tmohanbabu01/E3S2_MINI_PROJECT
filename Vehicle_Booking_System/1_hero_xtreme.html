About Xtreme 160R
Hero Xtreme 160R is a street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,17,920 in India.
It is available in 7 variants and 5 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,23,875.
The Hero Xtreme 160R is powered by 163cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 15 bhp and a torque of 14 Nm.
With both front and rear disc brakes, Hero Xtreme 160R comes up with anti-locking braking system.
This Xtreme 160R bike weighs 139.5 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.
The Xtreme 160R is Heroâ€™s newest entrant in the 150-160cc sporty commuter segment in India.
The motorcycle is a replacement to the now-discontinued Xtreme Sports which failed to tug at the heartstrings of many buyers in the country during its existence.
The motorcycle sports a sharp styling that incorporates a radical-looking headlamp, beefy fuel tank and an upswept and sporty-looking tail section.
On the design front, it is based on the Xtreme 1.R concept which Hero had showcased at the EICMA 2019 in Milan.
