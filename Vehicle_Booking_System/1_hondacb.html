About CB300F
Honda CB300F is a super bike available at a starting price of Rs. 2,26,220 in India.
It is available in 2 variants and 3 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 2,29,220
. The Honda CB300F is powered by 293.52cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 23.8 bhp and a torque of 25.6 Nm.
With both front and rear disc brakes, Honda CB300F comes up with anti-locking braking system.
This CB300F bike weighs 153 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 14.1 liters.

Honda 2Wheelers India has launched the CB300F in our market to compete in the 300cc roadster segment.
This motorcycle is available in two variants – Deluxe and Deluxe Pro – and the differences are limited to the Bluetooth module.
All other specifications and features are identical on both variants.
